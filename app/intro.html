GOALS!

People with goals tend to out-perform those without goals. The goals you set and your beliefs about these goals guide your behaviours and make it purposeful. Goals should be challenging but not seem unreachable. This way you get the most out from completing your goals. A survey about goals was carried out in Harvard University where students were asked about their current goals and objectives. 84% of participants hadn't set any goals. 13% of students had some goals and only 3% of the students had certain goals and objectives planned out. The same survey was carried out again 10 years later and the same participants were queried about their monthly wages. The same 84% mostly earned and anverage wage. The 13% earned two times the average wage. The 3% earned 10 times the wages of other groups combined. See how planning goals may have a great impact on you life?
