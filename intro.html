WELCOME

A survey about goals was carried out in the Estonian Business School where they asked students about their current goals and objectives.
83% of participants had no goals set. 16% of students had some goals and only 1% of the students had certain goals and objectives planned out. The same survey was carried out again 10 years later and the same participants were queried about their monthly wages. The same 86% mostly earned an average wage. The 16% earned 3 times the average wage. The 1% earned 10 times more than the other groups wages combined.
